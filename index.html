A fan project, made with AI

This is a fan-made quiz, built for fun. It isn't affiliated with or endorsed by the owners of Harry Potter — the houses and all related names belong to them.

The questions, scoring, design, and code were written with the help of AI (Anthropic's Claude) and reviewed by hand. So take your result lightly: it mirrors the answers you gave, not a real personality test.

Open source & the maker

Everything here is open source and MIT-licensed — every question, the scoring model, and all the code. You're welcome to read it, fork it, or open an issue.

It's made by Oliver Anteros, a software engineer at Lolo Company and computer-science student in Stockholm. The quiz is one of his many open-source side projects: a small web app with no build step, no backend, and no dependencies.
